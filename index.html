Pac-Men

In this project I showcase the use of JavaScript to dynamically manipulate the DOM moving the Pac-Man heads around in a pre-determined area.

Googly-Eyes

Live Bus Tracker

This project uses asynchronous functions to continually fetch real time data and utilize this to manipulate the DOM moving a marker to an updated position each time.

About Me

My name is Diego Berumen and I’m a tireless learner with a passion for multimedia and design technologies. I began working in graphic design many years ago. When I design a website, I work to achieve balance between elegance, simplicity and functionality. My goal is to create designs that will inspire and are timeless. I have worked with many of the popular languages such as HTML, CSS, and JS.

My interests go beyond graphic design. To further my knowledge and build on my existing skills, I am currently a student of the prestigious MIT xPro. I know learning from industry experts will build foundational knowledge for my career.
